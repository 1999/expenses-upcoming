## MODIFIED Requirements

### Requirement: Navigation to Yearly View

The Navigation Bar in the monthly expense page SHALL allow users to navigate to the yearly overview.

#### Scenario: Clickable year in title

- **WHEN** the user is on the `MonthlyExpensePage`
- **THEN** the year portion of the Navbar title SHALL be interactive.
- **AND** when clicked, it SHALL navigate the user to the corresponding yearly view (`/:year`).
